### Resources Read via Agent-Reach:

1. **Option Alpha - Options Greeks** (optionalpha.com/learn/options-greeks)
   - Delta: 0-1 for calls, -1-0 for puts
   - Gamma: Rate of change of delta
   - Theta: Time decay per day
   - Vega: Sensitivity to IV change
   - Rho: Sensitivity to interest rates

2. **Option Alpha - Iron Condor Strategy** (optionalpha.com/strategies/iron-condor)
   - Sell OTM call spread + sell OTM put spread
   - Max profit: Total credit received
   - Max loss: Width - credit
   - Time decay benefits position
   - IV decrease benefits position

3. **Option Alpha - Bull Put Credit Spread** (optionalpha.com/strategies/bull-put-credit-spread)
   - Sell put + buy lower put
   - Bullish outlook
   - Max profit: Credit received
   - Max loss: Width - credit
   - Adjust by adding bear call spread (iron condor)

4. **Option Alpha - Options Trading for Beginners** (optionalpha.com/learn/options-trading-for-beginners)

   - Extrinsic vs intrinsic value

### Key Insights from Agent-Reach Research:

1. **Options are derivatives** that derive value from underlying assets
2. **One contract = 100 shares** (contract multiplier)
3. **Intrinsic value** = max(0, stock price - strike price) for calls
4. **Extrinsic value** = time value + volatility premium
5. **Time decay accelerates** as expiration approaches
6. **IV crush** at earnings = profit for sellers
7. **Adjustments** can extend or modify positions
8. **Rolling** moves to later expiration for more credit
